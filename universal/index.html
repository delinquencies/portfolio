Quick Rules of Thumb

Prescription? Use Y22 or Y20 first. No squinting, no stacking bad fits on top of bad fits.
Wet / Dust / Splash? Move into goggles. G472AFC for splash. CX4080 for premium anti-fog and long wear.
Sharp + Impact? Choose GX7, GX9, or HEX-3013IMP. If impact is not needed, use GX1.
Welding? Go straight to GX11 or Blue Beast. Do not treat general cut gloves like welding gloves.
Outdoor Head Protection? Full brim beats cap style when weather and sun exposure start throwing elbows.
Noise Choice LPF-1 for disposable, U-260090 for corded, BH9010C for over-ear.
